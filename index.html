Medical Testing Services

Allergy

Laboratory offers allergy testing for common allergic reaction triggers and for occupational allergens, including chemicals, insects and drugs. To help our patients find out if they are allergic to a particular substance, Laboratory tests for more than 50 different allergens – such as food, animal dander, pollens, mould, trees, weeds, grasses and insect venom. We provide a traditional skin prick test performed by an Allergist, and a blood test, where a small amount of blood is drawn and the level of allergen-specific antibodies is measured
